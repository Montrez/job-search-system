🛡️ Anti-Double Submit Protocol

Before any recruiter submits your application, require them to confirm:
1. The exact company name and state/location
2. The specific job title and req ID if available
3. That no other recruiter has already submitted you

Rule: If you can't get a company name confirmation, do not allow submission. Track which recruiter submitted you to which company in the tracker's "Submitted By" field.

📊 Weekly Check-In Process

Every week, spend ~60 minutes on your job search:

✅ Review overdue follow-ups → send catch-up emails
✅ Check responses tab → move conversations forward
✅ Add any new contacts from LinkedIn
✅ Update status of all active applications
✅ Set follow-up dates for anything without one
✅ Review criteria — are you applying to the right roles?
